    Cricket

    Cricket is a popular bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of eleven players each. Originating in England, it has a rich history and is now played worldwide, particularly in countries like India, Australia, Pakistan, and South Africa. The game is played on a circular or oval-shaped field with a 22-yard-long pitch at the center. Teams take turns to bat and bowl, with the batting side aiming to score runs by hitting the ball and running between wickets, while the bowling side tries to dismiss the batsmen and limit runs. Matches can vary in format, from the traditional five-day Test matches to the fast-paced Twenty20 games. Cricket is known for its strategic depth, sportsmanship, and the passionate following of its fans.

    Security

    Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computer systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, unauthorized access, and damage. It involves a range of technologies, processes, and practices designed to safeguard information integrity, confidentiality, and availability. Cybersecurity measures include the use of firewalls, encryption, anti-virus software, intrusion detection systems, and multi-factor authentication. As cyber threats such as malware, phishing, ransomware, and hacking become increasingly sophisticated, cybersecurity is crucial for protecting sensitive information and maintaining the trust and functionality of digital infrastructures across businesses, governments, and personal users. Effective cybersecurity strategies also involve regular updates, security awareness training, and incident response planning
